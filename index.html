Lock In

All students today face distractions from short form media consumption, Lock In offers a solution by transforming studying into a fun, engaging experience. The platform uses a timer to encourage sustained focus, incorporates competitiveness to drive motivation, and integrates short-form educational content to keep learning interesting without distractions. A collaborative forum lets students ask and answer questions while earning digital rewards, fostering a supportive community. By combining focus, competition, and collaboration, Lock In helps students overcome their modern challenges. This project was made by me and Pratham Bansal to submmit to the Steam Showcase at Vernon Hills High School. We won second in the tech section of the contest.
