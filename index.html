Lock In

All students today face distractions from short form media consumption, Lock In offers a solution by transforming studying into a fun, engaging experience. The platform uses a timer to encourage sustained focus, incorporates competitiveness to drive motivation, and integrates short-form educational content to keep learning interesting without distractions. A collaborative forum lets students ask and answer questions while earning digital rewards, fostering a supportive community. By combining focus, competition, and collaboration, Lock In helps students overcome their modern challenges.
